About Me

Hi! I am Chang Yue. I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Dynamic Graphics Project lab at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Eitan Grinspun. In general, I am interested in physical simulation in computer graphics. I am currently doing some projects on speeding up deformable simulation using neural networks. Before my Ph.D study, I got my master's degree from School of Software & Microelectronics, Peking University, where I was lucky to work with Prof. Xiaowei He, Prof. Sheng Li and Prof. Guoping Wang on solid-fluid coupling. I got my bachelor's degree from Beihang University in 2019.
